Objective

For this term project I plan to inform those who have come to Massachusetts, specifically the Newton and Brookline area, of pizza shops that I have personally enjoyed and would recommend. The website I will be creating allows people to read about the different pizza styles and creations offered across those shops along with my go-to items. I plan to do this by writing about 4 different pizza shops, and including sub pages for pictures and my favorite slice from each shop.

Target Audience

The target audience for my website is those who come to the Brookline/Newton area either for work or school and are craving a quality pizza.
