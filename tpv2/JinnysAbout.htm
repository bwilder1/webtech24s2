About Jinny's Pizzeria


About

Jinny's is a local pizzeria located in Newton Center. Lydia Reichert named the pizzeria after her grandmother, Jinny. Lydia shares that she came to love food because of her grandmother's cooking, which led to her decision to be a chef. Lydia had been working with Dave Punch and Shane Smyth in the culinary field and subsequently partnered with them when opening Jinnys. Lydia's love for pizza is shown through the delicious wood-fired pizzas that they create. I hope you all have a chance to visit and indulge in their tasty pizzas.

Pizza Style

Jinny's pizza takes on a classic neapolitan style. All of the pizzas are made in their Valoriani oven. That oven is a 4,000 lb wood-fired behemoth that cooks the pizza at 700 degrees in order to give the dough a light crisp while keeping the crust fluffy. The standard neapolitan pizza is cooked at 800 degrees, so theirs is a bit more doughy than a standard neapolitan pizza. All pizzas come in a 12 inch size that is cut up into six slices.
