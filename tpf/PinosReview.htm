Review of Pinos Pizza


Slices

Pinos pizza is a place that offers individual slices. Many people working within the Brighton area come here for a quick bite, so it's smart to have slices readily available for people on the go. Once a slice is selected, they throw it in the oven to heat it back up to ensure maximum enjoyment. I noticed that many of their pizzas include vegetables as toppings, the main ones being olives, tomatoes, and peppers. I have had a vegetable slice before, but it's not my go to like a pepperoni slice is. Although I won't be reviewing the sicillian, I highly recommend you try their BBQ Chicken sicillian slice, as it is one of my favorites there.

Review

I'm going to try to keep this review as one without bias, but it's going to be hard given the impact this place has had on my life. I got a fresh pizza so my review conditions can be on par with the others. I got a pepperoni slice, and unlike Pepe's, their pepperoni is sliced wider and thinner. I will say it's good, but I prefer Pepe's pepperoni. The best part about this pizza is the cheese to sauce ratio, as there will never be a bite with an overwhelming amount of either. The dough was thin and pretty crispy, but not as crispy as Pepe's. The crust is by far my favorite with a crunchy outside but soft and doughy inside. Overall this pizza is very good, and if you want it in the best condition, get a whole pizza rather than a reheated slice, trust me.

Final Score

8.7
